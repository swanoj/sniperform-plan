## TEMPLATE 1: HERO VIDEO CREATIVE BRIEF

**Project:** Sniperform USA — Paid Social Campaign  
**Client:** Oliver (Sniperform)  
**Timeline:** Due by [DATE]  
**Deliverable:** 1 vertical video (9:16), 15–30 seconds  

### Objective
Drive awareness and conversions for Sniperform Level Up Performance Grips (patented bilateral balance feedback).

### Hook (First 3 Seconds — CRITICAL)
**Copy:** "Thicker grips = bigger arms"  
**Visual:** Split screen or dynamic text overlay on person lifting

**Goal:** Stop the scroll. Make user watch.

**Tone:** Authoritative, factual, educational

### Results (Seconds 15–28)
**Visual:** Before/after or muscle activation comparison  
- Person flexing arm (muscle definition)
- Or: Split screen showing weak grip vs. strong grip moment
- Or: Customer testimonial (if available)

**Copy:**
"That's why 5,000+ serious lifters switched. See the difference."

**Tone:** Confident, social proof

### CTA (Last 2 Seconds)
**Visual:** Logo animation + landing page URL  
**Copy:** "Learn how" or "See the science"  
**Button/Link:** sniperform.com/[landing-page]  
**Tone:** Soft, not pushy

### Sound Design
**Option A (Recommended for Instagram):**
- Voiceover (male, authoritative, not high-energy)
- Music: Dark, modern beat (minimal, lets voiceover shine)
- SFX: Metallic clangs (light, not overdone), heartbeat (subtle)

**Option B (for TikTok):**
- Music: Current trending audio (check TikTok top sounds)
- Captions: All dialogue on-screen (many watch silent)
- SFX: Minimal, let music carry it

### Visual Style
- **Lighting:** Dark gym environment (matches brand aesthetic)
- **Color grading:** High contrast, emphasize teal accent colors
- **Aspect ratio:** 9:16 (vertical video for Stories/Reels/TikTok)
- **Product visibility:** Spirit level vial must be visible/prominent every scene
- **Real gym setting:** Not studio, not white background (authentic)

### Duration Variations
- **15 seconds:** Hook + Proof + CTA (fast-paced, high energy)
- **30 seconds:** Hook + Proof + Results + CTA (comprehensive, educational)
- **60 seconds:** Full story arc (education-focused, long-form)

**Primary:** 30 seconds  
**Secondary:** 15 seconds (for Stories)  
**Optional:** 60 seconds (YouTube pre-roll)

### Performance Goals
- **CTR target:** 2–3%+
- **Watch-through rate:** 50%+ (avg person watches half)
- **CPC target:** $0.80–1.20
- **ROAS target:** 0.50x+ (first week), scale if 0.75x+

### Revision Notes
- Emphasize bilateral balance (Sniperform's differentiator — Fat Gripz can't copy)
- Show spirit level vial in action (unique feature, not just thick grip)
- Use real gym footage (no stock footage)
- Avoid: Overly hype-y, bro-science, cheesy music

## TEMPLATE 4: BLOG POST BRIEF

**Project:** Sniperform Blog — Educational Content  
**Format:** Long-form article, 1,500–2,000 words  
**Due:** [DATE]  
**Platform:** sniperform.com/blog  

### Title
[Main keyword] — [Benefit or angle]

**Examples:**
- "The Complete Science Behind Thick Grip Training"
- "How to Add 20 Lbs Per Arm With Thick Grips"
- "The Hidden Problem With Your Grip Strength"

### SEO Target Keywords
Primary: [Main keyword]  
Secondary: [Related keywords]  

Example:
- Primary: "thick grip training"
- Secondary: "arm size", "grip strength", "barbell training"

### Outline (Structure)
1. **Intro (150 words)**
   - Hook: Why this matters
   - Preview what reader will learn
   - Establish authority

2. **Section 1: The Problem (300 words)**
   - Describe common issue
   - Why most lifters miss it
   - Cost of not knowing

3. **Section 2: The Science (400 words)**
   - Explain mechanism clearly
   - Use analogies/examples
   - Cite research if available

4. **Section 3: How to Apply (300 words)**
   - Practical steps reader can take
   - Specific exercises
   - Frequency/volume guidelines

5. **Section 4: Expected Results (200 words)**
   - Timeline (week 1, month 1, month 3)
   - Specific metrics
   - Customer example if available

6. **Conclusion + CTA (250 words)**
   - Summary of key points
   - Call to action (product link)
   - Soft pitch, not aggressive

### Tone & Style
- **Audience:** Intermediate to advanced lifters (25–55, goal-oriented)
- **Tone:** Authoritative, educational, conversational (not academic)
- **Voice:** First-person perspective preferred ("I recommend", "I've seen", "We believe")
- **Vocabulary:** Balance technical terms with explanations
- **Length:** Target 1,500–2,000 words (Google loves long-form)

### Content Requirements
- [ ] Clear headline
- [ ] Meta description (155 chars)
- [ ] Internal links (2–3 to other blog posts or product page)
- [ ] External links (1–2 to credible sources)
- [ ] High-quality images (1–2, optimized)
- [ ] Subheadings (H2s) for scanability
- [ ] Bold key phrases
- [ ] Call-to-action button (bottom of post, link to landing page)
