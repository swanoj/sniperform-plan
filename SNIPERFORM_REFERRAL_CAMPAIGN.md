## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

**Referral campaigns are one of the highest-ROI marketing tactics for fitness products.** Existing customers trust the product, have used it successfully, and are motivated to share with their network (gym buddies, coaches, training partners).

**Sniperform Referral Opportunity:**
- **Current customer base:** ~100–200 customers (estimated)
- **Potential referral network:** 5–10 referrals per customer (conservative)
- **Potential revenue from referrals:** $3,000–$12,000 per month (by Month 3)
- **Cost per referral:** $20 (incentive) vs. typical $40–60 CAC from paid ads
- **Lifetime value of referred customers:** Higher (trust-based acquisition, lower churn)

**Why this works for Sniperform:**
1. Product solves a specific problem (bilateral balance) that lifters talk about
2. Real results (arm growth, strength gains, symmetry) are visible and shareable
3. Target audience (strength athletes) actively network and recommend gear
4. Low friction (digital product, easy to share)
5. High perceived value relative to price ($59.95)

## PART 1: REFERRAL MECHANICS

### Basic Structure

**Incentive Model:** Both-sides rewards (customer + referee both get discount)

```
EXISTING CUSTOMER (Referrer)
├─ Action: Share referral link with friend
├─ Reward: $20 off next purchase
└─ Trigger: Referee completes purchase

FRIEND (Referee)
├─ Action: Click link, buy Sniperform grips
├─ Reward: $15 off first purchase (new customer discount)
└─ Tracking: Unique URL parameter (utm_source=referral&utm_ref=[unique_id])
```

### Why This Incentive Structure Works

| Party | Incentive | Value Prop | Motivation |
|-------|-----------|-----------|-----------|
| **Referrer** | $20 off next purchase | Double pack + free shaker ($100 bundle → $80) | Upsell + retention |
| **Referee** | $15 off first purchase | Product ($59.95 → $44.95) | Removes price objection for cold referral |
| **Sniperform** | Net: -$35 per referral | CAC: $35 vs. $50–60 from paid ads | Profitable customer acquisition |

## PART 4: TRACKING & MEASUREMENT

### Referral Metrics Dashboard

Track these KPIs weekly:

| Metric | Formula | Target (Month 1) | Target (Month 3) |
|--------|---------|------------------|------------------|
| **Referral signups** | Unique links clicked | 50–100 | 300–500 |
| **Referral conversion rate** | Referral buys ÷ referral clicks | 20–30% | 25–35% |
| **Referral revenue** | # conversions × order value | $1,200–$1,800 | $7,500–$12,000 |
| **CAC from referrals** | Total referral incentive ÷ # customers | $20–25 | $20–25 |
| **Referral repeat rate** | Customers who make 2+ referrals | 10–15% | 30–40% |
| **Average referrals per customer** | Total referrals ÷ # customers | 0.5–1.0 | 1.5–2.5 |
| **LTV of referred customers** | Repeat purchase rate of referee | 15–25% | 35–50% |

### Setup in Analytics

**Google Analytics 4:**
```
Custom dimension: utm_ref=[customer_id]
Event tracking: referral_signup, referral_conversion
Goal: Track referred purchases separately from organic/paid
```

**Shopify:**
```
Custom metafield on customer: referrer_customer_id
Automation: When referral purchase completes, trigger:
  1. Apply $15 discount to referee (one-time)
  2. Apply $20 discount to referrer (next purchase)
  3. Send email confirmation to both parties
```

**Email Platform (Klaviyo/ConvertKit):**
```
Segment: Customers with 0 referrals, 1+ referrals, top referrers
Personalization: Show personalized earnings, unique link, referral count
A/B testing: Test subject lines, CTAs, incentive amounts
```
