## Key Decisions & Guardrails

1. **Don't pause campaigns before 2 weeks of data**
   - Too early to call winner/loser
   - Each campaign needs ~500 conversions minimum to be statistically significant

2. **Only scale if ROAS > 0.75x**
   - If ROAS drops below 0.50x on any campaign, pause it + investigate
   - Test creative/audience changes, don't just throw more budget at it

3. **Always test before scaling**
   - No "hero" campaign at scale without 2+ weeks of 0.75x+ ROAS proof
   - Test at $30–50/day minimum before daily budget of $150+

4. **Creative refresh every 2–3 weeks**
   - Ad frequency > 3 = creative fatigue
   - Rotate new variations in weekly

5. **Mobile-first mindset**
   - 70%+ of traffic will be mobile
   - All assets tested on mobile first, desktop second
