🎯 Strategy Overview

Core Promise

"The first gym grip that tells you when you're cheating yourself."

Sniperform's patent (bilateral balance vial) is the differentiator. Fat Gripz can't copy this. Use it relentlessly in all marketing.

The 4 Science Pillars (Use in ALL Content)

  1. Irradiation — Thick grip → ↑ grip activation → radiates neural drive up arm → bigger biceps/triceps
  2. Neural Inhibition — CNS limits force on thin bars; thick grips bypass this protective mechanism
  3. Weak Link Theory — Grip is often the bottleneck in arm training; removing it allows muscles to work harder
  4. Bilateral Balance (Sniperform-exclusive) — Spirit level reveals imbalance in real time, prevents compensation patterns
✅ Why This Matters for USA Market: The USA strength training market is highly competitive but unsaturated on the balance angle. Most lifters know thicker grips work, but have NO IDEA their left/right strength is imbalanced. That's Sniperform's differentiator.
