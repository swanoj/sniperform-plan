## 5. FUNNEL ANALYSIS

**Landing page traffic flow:**

```
Step                  | Sessions | % of Visitors | Drop-off |
Visitor arrival       | 1,000    | 100%          | —        |
↓
View product          | 950      | 95%           | 5%       |
↓
Add to cart           | 50       | 5%            | 95% 🚨   |
↓
Initiate checkout     | 35       | 3.5%          | 30%      |
↓
Complete purchase     | 5–8      | 0.5–0.8%      | 75%      |
```

**Biggest opportunities:**
1. **Add-to-cart drop-off (95%):** Messaging issue? Price objection? Trust concern?
   - Recommendation: A/B test hero copy, add social proof, reduce friction
2. **Checkout drop-off (30%):** Form too complex? Mobile issue? Payment processor problem?
   - Recommendation: Audit checkout on tablet, simplify form, verify Shopify integration
3. **View product (95% ✅):** Good — landing page headline is working

## HOW TO USE THIS DASHBOARD

1. **Every Monday morning:**
   - Pull data from Meta Ads Manager (last 7 days)
   - Pull data from Google Analytics 4 (last 7 days)
   - Pull data from Shopify (last 7 days)
   - Fill in this template

2. **During Monday check-in call:**
   - Share dashboard with Madigix
   - Review top performers
   - Discuss underperformers
   - Make decisions on pauses/increases/tests

3. **Track trends:**
   - Week-over-week changes (is cost going up? conversions down?)
   - Cumulative progress toward targets
   - Red flags (tablet rendering bug, creative fatigue, etc.)

4. **Share with stakeholders:**
   - Monthly summary for Oliver + investors/team
   - Keep it simple (high-level numbers, key insights)
